Advantages

Tri-tech allows you to get a much better feel for things you have an interest in, and how to prepare for careers in that field. This makes it much easier to get a job is the field you want at a younger age, or at least understand how you need to prepare. Overall, it’s a great advantage for starting off out of high school.

What I Learned

This year at tri-tech I have come to better understand much more about hardware, several coding languages (I especially liked working with python), setting up networks, and other technological skills that I’ll be able to apply to a job and my own systems at home. This year has also helped me learn how to better work with a group in projects. Sometimes it moves a bit fast, but if you are paying attention and really trying to learn what’s being taught, it’s a very good program.
